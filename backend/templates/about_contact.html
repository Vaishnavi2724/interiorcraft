About InteriorCraft

InteriorCraft is a web-based interior design tool that allows users to create, visualize and save their room designs using an interactive editor.

Users can drag and drop furniture, customize layouts, and manage multiple design projects easily. Our goal is to make interior designing simple, creative and accessible for everyone.

Key Features

  • Drag & Drop Furniture Editor
  • Save and Load Multiple Room Designs
  • Furniture Catalog for Inspiration
  • User Authentication and Design Management
